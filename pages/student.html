Mashup

Mashup was one of my first design assignments. The task was to create a visual mashup of 2 songs.

I listened to my two chosen songs, and sketched several different types of lines that looked like the songs sounded. After refining the best of the sketches, I arranged the scans of the chosen thumbnails in indesign until I felt I had a final cohesive design.

Finaly, I drew out the final design on Bristol Paper using black micron pens.
